About Us

EDUKATEE has been started considering the less time and attention provided in the school for students. It is platform where we connect the students and the teacher on a similar platform and to elevate the students from the present academic standard to the next level and to provide a strong foundation to conceptual learning.

How it Works

Classes are usually held on the Zoom Video platform. Each class will have a maximum of 4 students. Your child will have access to plenty of resources and our custom dashboard, where all assignments are posted. Assignments will also be uploaded through the Dashboard. Parents will have frequent Parent-Teacher Meets and access  reports from the teachers to know their child's progress throughout the year.

What do we teach?

We teach Math,English,Sciences,Economics,Business Studies and Language Subjects in both IGCSE,CBSE and ICSE boards. We teach all grades.
